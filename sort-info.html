The 3 R's: A Foundation

Before sorting, the waste hierarchy guides us:

Reduce

The most effective step. Choose products with minimal packaging and opt for durable goods over disposable ones. Think unpackaged fruits and vegetables, refillable containers, and buying in bulk.

Reuse

Before discarding, consider if an item can be used again. Glass jars become storage, old t-shirts become cleaning rags, and furniture can be repaired or upcycled. A little creativity can give an item a whole new life.

Recycle

The final step. This transforms materials into new products, but only when done correctly‚Äîcontamination breaks the chain. Clean and dry materials are key to a successful recycling process.
