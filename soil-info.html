The Alchemy of Decomposition

Composting is nature's recycling program‚Äîa precise dance of carbon, nitrogen, oxygen, and water. Understanding this balance transforms kitchen scraps into black gold.

Carbon (Browns)

Dry leaves, paper, cardboard‚Äîthe energy source that feeds the decomposition process and provides structure. Browns are the slow-burning fuel for your compost pile.

Nitrogen (Greens)

Fresh kitchen scraps, grass clippings‚Äîthe protein that builds microbial communities and activates decomposition. Greens are the fast-acting ingredients that get your compost cooking.
